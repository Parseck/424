About 424

FourToFour, Software Engineering and Ethical Hacking.

FourToFour is a daughter company of Jaylbreak Software and the greater Mason Consolidated corporation. Created in the basement of an annoyed mother, FourToFour capitalizes on it's employee's love of programming, and knowledge of ethical hacking, to provide a cheaper alternative to the big company's IT guys and programming teams. We also do web design. In fact, this website was written in HTML5 entirely by the CEO and first employee of FourToFour.

FourToFour is also the main outlet of Jaylbreak Software products. Inventory, base pricing, and more, is updated weekly in the Storefront. Jaylbreak Software products have non-negotiable prices, however they are avalible for instant download, whereas FourToFour products are negotiable in price but the time frame is more flexable.
